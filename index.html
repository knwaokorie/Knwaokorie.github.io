The Negative Effects of the Excessive Consumption of Fast Food

Summary of Project

The research deals with the properties of fast food that make it unhealthy for excessive consumption. It has been found that several chemicals that are undigestable have been found in fastfood products, but the fast food industry remains prominent due to its marketing procedures and its easy accesibility.

The research then argues that there is a serious health problem in the United States by detailing the causes of the increase in obesity rates. A serious U.S. health problem is an unexplained increase in obesity. Using state and large-market data, diet’s role is examined by relating diet differences and obesity differences across areas. Results suggest that the obesity problem is not due to recent changes in food consumed at home or away.
